About Fernando Alonso

Fernando Alonso is a Spanish Formula One driver currently competing for Aston Martin. He is a two-time World Champion and one of the most talented drivers in the history of the sport.

Career Highlights

  • 2005: Became the youngest double world champion at the age of 25.
  • 2006: Defended his title, winning back-to-back championships.
  • 2018: Returned to Formula One after a two-year hiatus.
  • 2021: Joined the Alpine team and showcased his skill and experience.
